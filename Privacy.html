  <p>Snakey does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, postal address, or user account information.</p>

  <p>Users can manage their privacy choices from within the app.</p>


  <h2>5. Diagnostics</h2>

  <p>Third-party services used by Snakey, including Google AdMob, may collect crash logs, diagnostics, and performance information to maintain and improve service reliability.</p>


  <h2>6. Children's Privacy</h2>

  <p>Snakey does not knowingly collect personal information from children.</p>


  <h2>7. Support Website</h2>
