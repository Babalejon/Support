Privacy Policy for Snakey Fruits

Last updated: 2026-04-05

Snakey Fruits is developed and published by Fredrik Lejon Söder.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the Snakey Fruits app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Snakey Fruits does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, postal address, or user account information.

Snakey Fruits does not require account creation or user login.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Snakey Fruits stores certain data locally on your device only, including game settings, music and sound preferences, and local score or leaderboard data.

This local data is used only to support app functionality and is not sent to the developer.

3. Advertising

Snakey Fruits displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect certain data required to provide ads and support the advertising service, such as device identifiers, IP address, diagnostics, performance data, and advertising-related data.

Snakey Fruits is configured to use non-personalized ads.

For more information about how Google handles data, please visit Google Privacy Policy.

4. Consent and Privacy Choices

Where required by applicable law, Snakey Fruits uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent related to advertising and to provide privacy choices.

Users can manage their privacy choices from within the app.

5. Diagnostics

Third-party services used by Snakey Fruits, including Google AdMob, may collect crash logs, diagnostics, and performance information to maintain and improve service reliability.

6. Children's Privacy

Snakey Fruits does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Support Website

The Snakey Fruits support and privacy pages are static web pages and do not use analytics, advertising scripts, or tracking cookies.

8. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell personal information.

Data may be processed by third-party service providers used by the app, such as Google AdMob, only as necessary to provide their services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on the support website.
